Mastering the Art of Speed Cooking: Tips & Tricks

Want to become a speed-cooking pro? Here are a few tips and tricks to help you get dinner on the table in record time:

  • Meal Prep: Spend a few hours on the weekend prepping ingredients like chopping veggies, marinating meat, and cooking grains. This will save you tons of time during the week.
  • Use Pre-Cut Veggies: If you're really short on time, buy pre-cut veggies from the grocery store. It's a little more expensive, but it can be a lifesaver on busy weeknights.
  • Embrace One-Pot Meals: One-pot meals are a lifesaver for busy cooks. They minimize cleanup and are often quick and easy to prepare.
  • Cook Once, Eat Twice: Make a big batch of something on the weekend and eat it for dinner one night, then repurpose the leftovers for lunch or another dinner.
  • Utilize Your Appliances: Use your Instant Pot, air fryer, or slow cooker to make quick and easy meals. CookAI has recipes specifically designed for these appliances.

With a little bit of planning and preparation, you can become a weeknight dinner ninja! And CookAI will be your trusty sidekick, helping you to create delicious meals in minutes.
