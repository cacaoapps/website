Tips & Tricks for Air Fryer Success with CookAI

Want to become an air fryer pro? Here are some tips and tricks to help you achieve air fryer success with CookAI:

  • Don't overcrowd the basket: Make sure to leave enough space between the food items so the hot air can circulate properly. Overcrowding can lead to uneven cooking and soggy results.
  • Preheat your air fryer: Just like with an oven, preheating your air fryer helps ensure even cooking and crispy results.
  • Use a little oil: While air frying requires less oil than traditional frying, a light coating of oil can help promote browning and crisping.
  • Shake or flip the food halfway through cooking: This helps ensure even cooking on all sides.
  • Experiment with different temperatures and cooking times: Every air fryer is different, so it's important to experiment to find the perfect settings for your appliance. CookAI provides recommended temperatures and cooking times, but you may need to adjust them slightly based on your specific air fryer.

And remember, practice makes perfect! The more you use your air fryer and CookAI, the better you'll become at creating delicious and healthy meals.
