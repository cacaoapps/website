CookAI: Delete Account

This page provides information on how to delete your CookAI account, as required by Google Play. CookAI is developed by Greetext AI.

To delete your CookAI account, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to: support@greetextai.com
  2. Subject Line: Request to Delete CookAI Account
  3. Body of Email: Please clearly state your request to delete your CookAI account. For security purposes, specify the email address associated with your CookAI account.

Upon receiving your request, we will delete the following data associated with your account:

  • User Details: This includes your name, email address, and any other identifying information you provided during registration.
  • Preferences: This includes your dietary preferences, preferred cuisines, skill level settings, and any other personalized settings you configured within the app.
  • Recipes: This includes any recipes you created or saved within the CookAI app.

Please allow up to 10 business days for your account deletion request to be processed. You may receive a confirmation email once your account has been deleted. If you have any questions or encounter any issues, please don't hesitate to contact us at support@greetextai.com.
