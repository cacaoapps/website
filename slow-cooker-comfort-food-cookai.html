Slow Cooker Tips and Tricks

Ready to become a slow cooker pro? Here are a few tips to ensure slow cooker success:

  • Don't overfill your slow cooker: Aim to fill it no more than two-thirds full to allow for proper cooking and prevent spills. Nobody wants a slow cooker explosion – trust us.
  • Trim excess fat: While a little fat adds flavor, too much can make your dish greasy. Trim off any large chunks of fat from your meat before adding it to the slow cooker.
  • Don't lift the lid!: We know, it's tempting to peek, but every time you lift the lid, you release heat and increase the cooking time. Resist the urge and let the slow cooker work its magic.

With these simple tips, you'll be well on your way to creating slow cooker masterpieces that will impress your friends and family (and maybe even yourself!).
